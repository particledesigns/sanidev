Shipping & returns

02/12/15 - SERVICE UPDATE - Due to the exceptionaly high seasonal demand we are currently experiencing a delay of 24 hours on deliveries being shipped.

All orders received before 1.00pm will be shipped out the same day, any orders received after then will be shipped the following day. If your order is placed after 1.00pm on a friday, or over the weekend, your item will be shipped on the following Monday.

Our shipping rates are calculated based on the estimated weight and size of the items. We use services from DPD DIRECT, DPD EXPRESS, DPD CLASSIC, ROYAL MAIL SIGNED FOR and ROYAL MAIL AIRSURE for both domestic and international shipping.

INTERNATIONAL CUSTOMERS:

For all European & International purchases we will use DPD DIRECT, DPD EXPRESS, DPD CLASSIC, ROYAL MAIL SIGNED FOR and ROYAL MAIL AIRSURE dependent on your choice at the checkout phase of purchase. Each service requires a signature on delivery. For completely accurate shipping costs please view shipping costs at checkout.

ESTIMATED DELIVERY TIMES AND COSTS:

Please select which service you would like to use at the checkout. Estimated delivery times and exact costs are shown clearly at the checkout. Please check delivery times and costs per services available at the checkout. If you would like clarification on shipping prices or times please contact us at webstore@goodhood.co.uk.

Most destinations are shipped on a Delivery Duty Paid basis, which means that all import taxes and duties will be included in the final purchase price.

The following regions are sent on a Delivery Duty Paid basis:

United Kingdom

Europe

United States via DPD direct service

Australia via DPD direct service

New Zealand via DPD direct service

Please note the the Express service to United States, Australia & New Zealand is not a Delivery Duty Paid service. Unfortunately we currently do not offer Delivery Duty Paid services to regions not listed above. As the recipient, you are liable for all import duties, customs and local sales taxes levied by the country you are shipping to; payment of these is necessary to release your order. If you have any questions or queries please contact us at webstore@goodhood.co.uk.
